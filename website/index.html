The Wonders of Space Exploration

Humanity has always looked at the stars with curiosity. Space exploration is the physical exploration of outer space, both by human spaceflights and by robotic spacecraft. While the study of space is carried out mainly by astronomers with telescopes, the physical exploration of space is conducted both by unmanned robotic space probes and as well as by human spaceflight.

From the early days of the space race to the future of interstellar travel, we continue to push the boundaries of science and technology.
