Colonizing the Red Planet

Mars has long been the primary target for human colonization. With its thin atmosphere and frozen water, it is the most habitable planet in our solar system after Earth. Exploration of Mars has been ongoing for decades through robotic rovers like Curiosity and Perseverance.

SpaceX, led by Elon Musk, is developing the Starship to make life multi-planetary.

Related Missions

  • The SpaceX Starship is the key vehicle for Mars travel.
  • Mars missions often share technology with the Voyager Probes for deep space communication.
